Sweet Moments with Maynards

Maynards is known for creating some of the most playful and flavorful candies in the world. From classic Wine Gums to juicy Sour Patch Kids, every bite is a burst of fun. Perfect for movie nights, parties, or just a personal treat!

Maynards offers an experience that’s not just about candy, but about sharing memories with those around you. Whether you're indulging in a sweet candy party or giving a gift to a loved one, Maynards makes sure that every candy is packed with joy.

The History of Maynards Candy

Founded in the early 1900s, Maynards has evolved into one of the most beloved candy brands worldwide. Originally starting as a small confectionery business in Canada, the brand soon expanded to international markets, becoming synonymous with fun, flavor, and quality. Maynards is proud to maintain a tradition of deliciously fun candies with quality ingredients.

Throughout its journey, Maynards has been at the forefront of candy innovation, introducing new flavors, textures, and experiences. With a focus on creating unique and playful candies, Maynards has continued to bring smiles to faces of all ages.

Top Maynards Flavors

Maynards candies come in a wide range of vibrant and playful flavors. Here’s a list of some of our most popular options:

  • Swedish Candy: A chewy, fruity candy with a mix of delightful flavors in various shapes.
  • Swedish Cream: A smooth and creamy delight, perfect for fans of vanilla and caramel.
  • Fizzyfish Jellies: A fun, tangy, and fizzy candy shaped like fish.
  • Fuzzy Peach: A peach-flavored gummy with a sugary coating.
  • Cherry Blasters: Sweet and tart cherry-flavored jellies with a punch.
  • Gummy Bears: Classic fruity bears loved by all ages.
  • Juicy Squirts: Sour candy that releases a juicy, sweet filling.
  • Jujubes: A chewy, multi-flavored candy with a slightly tart kick.
